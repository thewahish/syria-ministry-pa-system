### Syrian Revolution Flag (CORRECT VERSION)
```
The Syrian Revolution/New Syrian flag MUST be used in all images showing Syrian national identity:
- TOP STRIPE: Green (#007A3D)
- MIDDLE STRIPE: White (#FFFFFF) with THREE RED STARS (#CE1126) arranged horizontally
- BOTTOM STRIPE: Black (#000000)
- Stars are 5-pointed, evenly spaced in the white stripe

NEVER use the old Assad-era flag (red-white-black with two green stars).
```
